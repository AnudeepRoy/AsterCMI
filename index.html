What are the types of Bone Marrow Transplant?

  • Autologous Bone Marrow Transplant - Marrow cells are collected from the patient's own body and transfused back after high dose chemotherapy
  • Allogenic Bone Marrow Transplant(Matched Sibling Donor) - Replacement of diseased of defective marrow with marrow from a genetically (HLA) matched sibling donor.
  • Halp-Identical Bone Marrow Transplant - Replacement of diseased or defected marrow from the parent.
